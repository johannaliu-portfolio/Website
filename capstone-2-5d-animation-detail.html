COMING SOON VIDEO

Illustration

The illustration pass establishes the visual mood board and key frame composition used to anchor the 2.5D scene style.

Asset separation

Foreground, midground, and background elements are separated into layered assets for depth control and parallax animation.

Rigging

Control handles and deformation guides are set up so each separated layer can be animated smoothly with consistent motion arcs.

Animation

Final timing and camera easing combine to deliver depth-rich 2.5D motion while preserving the original illustration style.

Summary

This 2.5D animation detail page documents the full workflow from visual concept to layered motion output, showing how illustration planning, asset separation, rig setup, and animation polishing come together in a cohesive final piece.
